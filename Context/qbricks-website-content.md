**Purpose:** the technical-but-accessible deep dive for the buyer and their architects. Still outcome-led, but with enough mechanism to satisfy a sceptical data leader.

### 2.1 Intro

**Heading:** QBricks — AI-enabled metadata management, built for governed enterprise data.

**Body:** QBricks is a sophisticated, governed and secure platform for managing metadata and for building and deploying data quality and ETL workflows. It works through two core constructs — Data Contracts and Data Products — and automates much of the metadata process so your teams can move faster without losing control or auditability.

### 2.2 How it works

Present as a numbered flow (this should also drive a diagram):

1. **Define** — Express data quality and ETL logic as **Data Contracts**. Each contract is explicit, owned and versioned.
2. **Compose** — Assemble contracts into **Data Products** that the business can find, understand and trust.
3. **Deploy** — A **single file** deploys both the infrastructure and the workloads — no multi-file sprawl, fewer errors, live in hours.
4. **Automate** — **Agents** take on routine metadata management, learn from your environment and continually improve — human always in the loop.
5. **Audit** — Every decision and its lineage is tracked. The entire process is auditable, end to end.

### 2.3 Core constructs

**Data Contracts** — the unit of governance. They make data quality and transformation logic explicit, owned and enforceable, and they can be browsed easily so teams share a single understanding.

**Data Products** — governed, discoverable assets the business can rely on, assembled from contracts and carrying their quality guarantees and lineage with them.
